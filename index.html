Case study

How a 150-person fintech recovered trust in their data in 6 weeks.

Fintech company, 150 employees

Series B · €40M ARR · 4 analysts, Power BI + Snowflake

The problem

The CEO stopped using BI dashboards — numbers differed from what Finance reported. Three departments used three different definitions of "conversion". Analysts spent 70% of time cleaning data, not analysing it. No one trusted the weekly report.

What we did

Week 1–2: BI Express Audit — mapped all data sources, found 4 metric conflicts, documented broken pipelines.

Week 3–5: Architecture Review — unified metric definitions (data contracts for 12 KPIs), redesigned the data layer in Snowflake, eliminated 3 manual Excel handoffs.

Week 6: Executive dashboard rebuilt. CEO back on BI within 2 weeks of go-live.
